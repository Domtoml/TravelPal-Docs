# Privacy Policy for TravelPal

**Effective Date:** 2024-05-20

**1. Introduction**
TravelPal ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your personal information when you use our mobile application ("App").

**2. Information We Collect**
*   **Account Information:** When you sign up, we collect your username and convert your password into a secure hash. We do NOT see or store your plain-text password.
*   **User Content:** We store the travel trips, plans, and itinerary items you create within the App.
*   **Extracted Data:** If you use our URL extraction feature (e.g., sharing a video link), we process that link to extract travel information (location, venue name). We do not track your browsing history outside of the specific links you choose to share with us.

**3. How We Use Your Information**
We use your information solely to:
*   Provide and maintain the App's functionality.
*   Allow you to create and manage your travel itineraries.
*   Process the travel links you submit.

**4. Data Storage and Security**
Your data is stored securely on cloud servers. We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS) for data in transit and secure hashing for passwords.

**5. Third-Party Services**
We may use third-party services (like Google Cloud Platform) to host our backend and database. These providers have access to your data only to perform tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for other purposes.

**6. Account Deletion**
You can request the deletion of your account and all associated data directly within the App via the "Profile" > "Delete Account" button. Upon confirmation, your data will be permanently removed from our active databases.

**7. Contact Us**
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@travelpal.app
